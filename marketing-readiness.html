How the Score is Calculated

Your score is computed the moment you complete the onboarding consultation and updates automatically as your profile and campaigns evolve.

1

Answers are weighted

Each answer to the consultation questions carries a point value. Specific, detailed answers earn more points than generic or skipped ones.

2

Signal scores are summed

The six signal scores are added together for a raw total out of 100. No single area can inflate your score — all six signals contribute.

3

Grade is assigned

Your total maps to one of four grades — Emerging, Growing, Advanced, or Elite — which determines the strategic approach your advisor takes with your account.

4

Score feeds the AI engine

Your readiness score directly influences the recommendations, growth plans, and AI insights generated in your dashboard. Higher scores unlock more advanced strategies.

5

Score evolves with you

As you add services, set budgets, and complete your profile, your score updates in real time. Elite status is achievable — it takes the right commitment across all six areas.

Common Questions

What businesses typically ask about the readiness score.

Is a low score bad?

Not at all. A lower score means there is more untapped opportunity. Most businesses that come to 6ix Developers score in the Growing or Emerging range — and that is exactly why they are here. Your score shows your advisor precisely where to focus first for the fastest results.

Can I improve my score?

Yes, and it improves automatically as your engagement with 6ix Developers deepens. Completing your profile, setting a budget, activating services, and adding competitors all raise your score without any extra effort on your part.

Does my score affect the services offered to me?

It influences the strategic focus your advisor applies — not which services are available. Every client has access to all five services regardless of score. However, your score helps your advisor prioritise where to invest your budget for maximum early impact.

How is this different from the Marketing Maturity Score in the dashboard?

The Readiness Score is calculated during onboarding from your self-reported answers. The Marketing Maturity Score in your dashboard is calculated from your live account activity — active services, tracked metrics, advisor engagement, and verified data. Think of Readiness as the starting line and Maturity as the ongoing measurement of progress.

Is the score shared with anyone?

Your score is visible to you and your assigned 6ix Developers advisor. It is never shared with third parties or used for any purpose outside your account.
