Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how the score works.

How often does my score update?

Your score updates in real time whenever you complete an area — fill in your profile, add a competitor, or have a service activated. You'll see the change immediately on your Overview page.

Can I reach 100 without all 5 services?

No. The Active Services pillar has a maximum of 25 points (8 per service), which requires more than 3 services to max out. To reach Elite (80+) you need at least 3 active services plus the other pillars mostly complete.

Is a lower score bad?

Not necessarily — it means there's untapped opportunity. Most businesses starting with 6ix Developers score in the Growing range (45–64). The score shows you exactly where to invest attention for the highest return.

Does my score affect how my advisor works with me?

Yes. Your advisor sees your score and uses it to prioritise their recommendations. A low Campaign Metrics score, for example, prompts your advisor to set up better tracking. Think of it as a shared dashboard between you and your advisor.

How is this different from my Growth Index?

The Marketing Maturity Score measures how well your marketing operation is set up. The Growth Index (shown in your KPI cards) reflects actual campaign performance — leads, traffic, ROAS. They're complementary: maturity predicts potential, the Growth Index measures results.
